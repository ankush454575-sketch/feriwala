# Application Workflow Guide

This document provides a step-by-step guide on how the application works for different user roles: regular users, resellers, and administrators. It outlines the key features and the sequence of actions each role can perform.

---

## 1. User Workflow

This section details the journey of a regular customer using the application.

### 1.1 Account Creation & Login

1.  **Sign Up:**
    *   Navigate to the application's homepage.
    *   Click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button.
    *   Fill in the required details (e.g., Name, Email, Password).
    *   Agree to the Terms and Conditions (if applicable).
    *   Click "Create Account".
    *   (Optional) Verify email address if a verification link is sent.
2.  **Login:**
    *   Navigate to the application's homepage.
    *   Click on the "Login" or "Sign In" button.
    *   Enter your registered Email and Password.
    *   Click "Login".
    *   (Optional) Use "Forgot Password" if you cannot access your account.

### 1.2 Browsing Products

1.  **View Categories:**
    *   From the homepage, browse available product categories (e.g., Electronics, Clothing, Books).
    *   Click on a category to view products within it.
2.  **Search Products:**
    *   Use the search bar at the top of the page.
    *   Type keywords related to the product you are looking for.
    *   Press Enter or click the search icon.
3.  **Filter & Sort:**
    *   Apply filters (e.g., price range, brand, ratings) from the sidebar or filter options.
    *   Sort products by relevance, price (low to high/high to low), or newest arrivals.
4.  **View Product Details:**
    *   Click on any product image or title to view its detailed page.
    *   Review product description, images, specifications, price, and customer reviews.

### 1.3 Adding to Cart

1.  **Select Quantity:**
    *   On the product detail page, choose the desired quantity.
2.  **Add to Cart:**
    *   Click the "Add to Cart" button.
    *   A confirmation message will appear, and the cart icon will update with the number of items.
3.  **Continue Shopping or View Cart:**
    *   You can continue browsing or click "View Cart" to proceed to checkout.

### 1.4 Checkout Process

1.  **Review Cart:**
    *   On the "Shopping Cart" page, review all items, quantities, and total price.
    *   (Optional) Update quantities or remove items.
2.  **Proceed to Checkout:**
    *   Click the "Proceed to Checkout" button.
3.  **Shipping Information:**
    *   Enter or select your shipping address.
    *   Choose a shipping method (if multiple options are available).
4.  **Payment Information:**
    *   Select a payment method (e.g., Credit Card, PayPal, COD).
    *   Enter payment details as required.
5.  **Order Review:**
    *   Review your entire order, including shipping, payment, and items.
    *   Confirm all details are correct.
6.  **Place Order:**
    *   Click "Place Order" to finalize your purchase.
    *   Receive an order confirmation message and email.

### 1.5 Order History & Tracking

1.  **Access Order History:**
    *   Go to "My Account" or "Profile".
    *   Click on "Order History" or "My Orders".
2.  **View Order Details:**
    *   Click on a specific order to view its details, including items purchased, shipping address, and payment information.
3.  **Track Order:**
    *   If available, click on the "Track Order" link to see the current status and shipping progress.

### 1.6 Profile Management

1.  **Edit Profile:**
    *   Go to "My Account" or "Profile".
    *   Update personal information (e.g., Name, Email, Phone Number).
    *   Save changes.
2.  **Manage Addresses:**
    *   Add, edit, or delete shipping and billing addresses.
3.  **Change Password:**
    *   Update your account password for security.

## 2. Reseller Workflow

This section outlines the functionalities available to approved resellers.

### 2.1 Account Creation & Login

1.  **Reseller Registration:**
    *   Navigate to the "Reseller Program" section (if separate) or "Sign Up" and select "Reseller Account".
    *   Fill in business details, contact information, and any required documentation.
    *   Submit the application for admin review.
2.  **Admin Approval:**
    *   Wait for the administrator to review and approve your reseller application.
    *   Receive an email notification upon approval.
3.  **Login:**
    *   Use your registered reseller credentials to log in, similar to a regular user, but you will have access to additional features.

### 2.2 Managing Inventory (Ordering in Bulk)

1.  **Access Reseller Dashboard:**
    *   After logging in, navigate to your "Reseller Dashboard" or "Wholesale Orders" section.
2.  **Browse Wholesale Products:**
    *   View products available for bulk purchase, potentially with special reseller pricing.
3.  **Place Bulk Order:**
    *   Select products and specify larger quantities.
    *   Add to a dedicated "Wholesale Cart".
    *   Proceed through a specialized bulk checkout process.

### 2.3 Tracking Reseller-Specific Orders

1.  **View Reseller Orders:**
    *   Access "My Orders" or "Reseller Order History" from your dashboard.
    *   View all bulk orders placed, their status, and associated invoices.
2.  **Order Status Updates:**
    *   Monitor the fulfillment and shipping status of your wholesale orders.

### 2.4 Reseller Dashboard

1.  **Overview:**
    *   View a summary of recent orders, sales performance, and available credit (if applicable).
2.  **Reports:**
    *   Access reports on purchase history, discounts applied, and potential earnings.

### 2.5 Communication with Admin/Support

1.  **Contact Support:**
    *   Use a dedicated contact form or messaging system to communicate with the admin team for inquiries, issues, or special requests.

## 3. Admin Workflow

This section describes the administrative functionalities for managing the entire application.

### 3.1 Admin Login

1.  **Access Admin Panel:**
    *   Navigate to the dedicated admin login URL (e.g., `yourdomain.com/admin`).
    *   Enter your administrator username and password.
    *   Click "Login".

### 3.2 User Management

1.  **View All Users:**
    *   Go to "Users" or "Customer Management".
    *   View a list of all registered users (regular customers and resellers).
2.  **Edit User Details:**
    *   Click on a user's profile to edit their information (e.g., name, email, password reset).
3.  **Manage Reseller Status:**
    *   Approve or reject reseller applications.
    *   Change a user's role to/from "Reseller".
4.  **Delete Users:**
    *   (Use with caution) Remove user accounts from the system.

### 3.3 Product Management

1.  **Add New Product:**
    *   Go to "Products" -> "Add New Product".
    *   Enter product name, description, price, category, stock quantity, images, and other attributes.
    *   Save the product.
2.  **Edit Existing Product:**
    *   View the list of products.
    *   Click on a product to modify its details.
    *   Update pricing, stock, descriptions, or images.
    *   Save changes.
3.  **Delete Product:**
    *   Remove products from the catalog.
4.  **Manage Categories:**
    *   Add, edit, or delete product categories.

### 3.4 Order Management

1.  **View All Orders:**
    *   Go to "Orders".
    *   View a list of all customer and reseller orders.
2.  **View Order Details:**
    *   Click on an order to see full details, including customer information, items, shipping, and payment status.
3.  **Update Order Status:**
    *   Change order status (e.g., Pending, Processing, Shipped, Delivered, Cancelled).
4.  **Process Refunds/Cancellations:**
    *   Initiate refunds or cancel orders as needed.

### 3.5 Inventory Management

1.  **Monitor Stock Levels:**
    *   View current stock for all products.
    *   Receive alerts for low stock items.
2.  **Adjust Stock:**
    *   Manually update stock quantities (e.g., for returns, new shipments).

### 3.6 Reporting and Analytics

1.  **Sales Reports:**
    *   Generate reports on sales performance, revenue, and popular products.
2.  **User Activity:**
    *   Monitor user registration trends and activity.
3.  **Reseller Performance:**
    *   Track reseller sales and contributions.

### 3.7 System Configuration

1.  **General Settings:**
    *   Configure application-wide settings (e.g., currency, tax rates, shipping options).
2.  **Email Templates:**
    *   Manage templates for transactional emails (order confirmation, shipping updates).
3.  **Payment Gateway Settings:**
    *   Configure and manage integrated payment gateways.
